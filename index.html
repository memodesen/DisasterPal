Project Description

Considering the unfortunate disasters such as earthquakes and forest fires in the world and in our country in recent years and examining first-hand how much we need to communicate with each other, we decided to create a platform that will draw more attention to the earthquake issue and lead our society to be more prepared for expected and possible disasters.

For this platform we decided to develop an application, DisasterPal, that enables users to carry out many activities from identifying those in need during a disaster, deciding what is needed in which areas, providing psychological support, and we can also find a friend to help us in a possible disaster, during or after a disaster. Our application offers a different usage interface for volunteers and disaster victims.

If we need to explain a little more about what it offers on the psychological side, we can say that it aims to bring free audio psychological help to people who are stranded in disasters or trying to cope with the traumas they experience after disasters. The locations of the disaster victims and the vehicles going to help can be seen from the application, so that individual help can be provided. As a result, this software aims to be a software that facilitates life psychologically, sociologically, and physiologically for both the disaster victims and those who go to help the disaster.
